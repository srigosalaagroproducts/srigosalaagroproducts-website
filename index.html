Products

Cocopeat

Cocopeat is a sustainable and versatile growing medium that is derived from coconut husk. It is commonly used in gardening and horticulture because of its numerous benefits.It is also added to the soil to enhance its drainage properties. Also, it is used on its own as a hydroponic growing medium. It is considered an eco-friendly and sustainable material.

Cocopeat Blocks (5 KG Blocks)

Coco peat block, also known as coir pith or coir dust, is made from coconut husks. We offer two variants: low EC and high EC. It serves as the ideal peat moss alternative in horticulture supplies. These are 100% soil-less growing mediums. It can be used for soil conditioning as well as a mulching agent.

Coconut coir Fibre

Coconut coir fibre is an ecologically friendly by-product of the coconut industry. Coconut coir is a natural fibre extracted from the husk of the coconut and is strong and durable. It is used in different products such as floor mats, door mats, brushes, and mattresses. Bale Coir Fibre is an excellent potting medium for starting seeds and potting plants.

Areca leaf plates, Areca leaf cups, Green leaf plates

We offer the most sustainable and environmentally friendly types of disposable plates and cups, which are healthy, hygienic, and eco-friendly. Which are offered in natural hues. And also, These are an excellent substitute for plastic or polymer-based plates and Cups since they decompose quickly and easily.

Organic Compost

Compost made from cocopeat is all-natural and has enhanced abilities for fostering the growth of agricultural and garden plants. It is also employed in the agricultural sector for the cultivation of a wide range of plants and crops, including garden-variety crops.

Potting mix

The blend made up of very high-quality substances that create a natural soil environment. It provides a conducive environment for different kinds of microbes like bacteria, fungi, and nematodes.Potting soils are better at retaining water and thus can remain moist for longer periods of time.It has Excellent air porosity and Can be used to grow any type of plants

Edible Oil

We are suppliers of a wide variety of premium-quality edible oils. We follow the traditional method to extract the wood- or cold-pressed oils with international standards like peanut oil, coconut oil, sesame oil, sunflower oil, safflower oil, extra virgin coconut oil, mustard oil, and castor oil by naturally crushing the oil seeds at room temperature. These oils are indeed healthier than any other oils as they retain their original nutrients and are also one of the essential commodities being used daily for cooking purposes.
